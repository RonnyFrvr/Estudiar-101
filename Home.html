    Summarizing

    Summarizing refers to simplifying all of your notes to make it shorter. Doing this you must highlight or keep the most important information as well as an explanation of the concept in your own words so you can further prove your understanding. I usually do this before exams, around 1 weeks before, but if you know you cannot write fast or study well enough start way before the exams if not whenever you come home everyday, summarize the info learnt that day.

    Repeat & Absorb

    This method involves you repeating the words you summarized in your head over and over again. Don't just repeat it, absorb it. By this I mean actually get to know the content by memorizing it. If this is too hard you can do the following:

  • Flash cards-
    • Write down some notes on small cards and shuffle them up then question yourself and answer
  • Imitate being a teacher
    • Imitate being a teacher by teaching someone what you learnt or teaching yourself by standing infont of the mirror and teaching yourself.
    There are many other ways one can absorb knowledge.

    Quiz

    This is the final part. The part in which you prepare to face the exams. You can prepare by revising or by asking someone to quiz you. Ensure they shuffle the questions so that it becomes harder on you and you learn from your mistakes.
